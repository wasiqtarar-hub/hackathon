Hi grandma. I hope heaven is treating you well. I have worked on a project that builds a website, you know those pages on the internet, like youtube, on which you used to watch lots of videos. Think of this project like building a 'Car Expert' that lives on the internet, ready to help you decide between two different vehicles without you having to flip through a hundred magazines.

First, I used a computer program called Visual Studio Code to write down a set of instructions. I taught the computer how to distinguish between cars based on simple things you’d see on a price tag: what year it was made, the company that built it (like you had a Toyota and I have a Volvo), the specific name of the car, and even the 'variant', which is just a fancy way of saying whether it’s the basic version or the one in which the side mirrors fold automatically.

To give this expert its intelligence, I had to go online and get a special digital 'key', called an API key. You can imagine this as a library card that grants the computer access to a very smart, invisible brain. I made sure to hide this key in a safe, private spot so that only my program could use it to think and answer questions.

Once the instructions were ready, I sent them to a secure online storage unit called Github. From there, I used a simple tool called Streamlit make the project visible to the public as a real website, meaning anyone in the world can open it on their phonr or computer.

Now, when you visit this website, you just pick two cars you’re interested in from a list. The program instantly gathers all the facts about them like how much gas they use or how many seats they have, and lays them out side by side. But the best part is the ADVICE at the bottom. The digital brain writes a simple summary explaining which car fits your life better. It might tell you that one is perfect for a big family with lots of groceries, while the other is better for someone who just wants a stylish, smooth ride for a Sunday drive. It’s like having a car-savvy friend available 24/7!
